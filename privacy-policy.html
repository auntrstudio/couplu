Data Storage

All data entered into Couplu is stored using:

  • SQLite database - Stored locally on your device
  • Device file system - For photos and attachments
  • iOS UserDefaults - For app preferences

No cloud storage. No external servers. No data transmission.

Third-Party Services

Couplu does NOT use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. There are no embedded SDKs that collect data.

Photo Library Access

If you choose to add photos to your gifts, events, or plans, Couplu requests access to your device's photo library. This access is:

  • Optional - You can use the app without photos
  • Local only - Photos are stored on your device
  • Never uploaded - Photos never leave your device
